Some Projects I've Worked On

Half Tyrone Away From Home

Runner Up Best Radio News Feature BJTC Awards 2014

'Half Tyrone Away From Home' tells the story of Irish emigration to London in the second half of the 20th century through the personal experience of Maggie Khondoker, owner of Maggie's Café and matriarch of the Irish community in Lewisham and beyond.

NTS Live From Dublin

I curated an 8 hour outside broadcast from All City Records in Dublin for NTS Radio showcasing some of the best DJs and acts from Ireland's capital. I also interviewed Irish Radio Legend Donal Dineen as part of the broadcast which can be streamed HERE

NTS @ MoMA PS1

I engineered a week of broadcasting from MoMA PS1 in New York for NTS showcasing artists from the city.

NTS Live From Glasgow

I engineered a 12 hour broadcast from Rubadub in Glasgow showcasing local artists.
